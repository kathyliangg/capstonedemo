why is staying organized important?

organization is an important skill that everyone should have! not being organized can lead to increased levels of stress and a lower quality of life. so, we created this stuffie organizer to help people build good organization habits while being able to organize their stuffies!

you can start good organization skills by taking time out of your day to make sure things go where they belong. for example, if you see something in your room that isn't where it is supposed to be, you could put it away immediately instead of having it build up! another way you could stay organized in your day to day life is to use organization apps. you could start by just using the notes app in your phone to plan out things you need to do or your schedule for the day. trust us, when you start doing this, life becomes so much easier and you'll never miss anything!
